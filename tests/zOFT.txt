Each Spoke Chain (EVM):
├── SETH.sol (OFT with 0.5% fee-on-transfer)
│   └── Fees sent to local FeeVault
├── Vault.sol (holds ETH/WETH collateral)
│   └── No fees here anymore (0% mint/burn)
│   └── Potentially relays ETH/WETH collateral to mainnet
│   └── Interactions limited to SETH contract
└── FeeVault.sol (accumulates SETH fees)
    └── sweepFees() → burn SETH for native token → Wormhole(?) to mainnet
    └── Interactions limited to SETH contract & Chainlink Automation

Solana:
├── SETH (SPL Token via LayerZero OFT)
│   └── Fees sent to local FeeVault PDA
├── Vault.sol (holds ETH/WETH collateral)
│   └── No fees here anymore (0% mint/burn)
│   └── Potentially relays ETH/WETH collateral to mainnet
│   └── Interactions limited to SETH contract
└── FeeVault (accumulates SETH fees)
    └── sweepFees() → burn SETH for ETH (Portal) → Wormhole to mainnet
    └── Interactions limited to SETH contract & Chainlink Automation

Mainnet:
├── SETH.sol (OFT - canonical)
│   └── Burn SETH for ETH → split fees & transfer
├── Vault.sol (holds ETH/WETH collateral)
│   └── No fees here anymore (0% mint/burn)
│   └── Potentially relays ETH/WETH collateral to mainnet
│   └── Interactions limited to SETH contract
├── FeeVault.sol (receives swept SETH from all chains)
│   └── sweepFees() → burn SETH for ETH → send to OVault
│   └── Interactions limited to SETH contract & Chainlink Automation
├── OVault.sol
│   └── Stores scalar balances & enables PBR claims
│   └── Enables multichain claims from a single liquidity source
│   └── No staking required. OVault calls 
├── AppRegistry.sol
│   └── Tracks TVL shares
│   └── Enables automated registration process
└── PBRManager.sol
    └── Collects tokenPrices[] and contractLiq[] using Chainlink Functions
    └── Calculates TVL proportions and distributes PBR as claimable scalars
